What exactly is Lifestyle photography?

First things first! When you search lifestyle photography, here's what comes up; 'Lifestyle photography is a kind of photography that mainly aims to capture portrait/people in situations, real-life events or milestones in an artistic manner and the art of the everyday. The primary goal is to tell stories about people's lives or to inspire people in different times.' For me this means not asking your kids to say 'cheese!' and instead just loving them hard and letting the session happen. We're after authentic moments with your favorite people after all.

Who is your client?

My clients are down to earth and trust that their everyday moments are beautiful and worth remembering. They don't mind a little chaos that goes hand in hand with family photography and they trust me to capture genuine laughter and tender moments instead of forced smiles. I'm there to document your perfect family just the way they are. To help you remember those fleeting moments whether you're a mama to be with a big beautiful belly, new parents just meeting your sweet baby, a family with wild and crazy little ones or one with moody teens. My clients are the best!!

What can I expect at a session?

Location has been decided on, outfits have been chosen and now it's go time! Don't worry if you're feeling nervous about it, I feel nervous every time we have our family pictures taken, just ask my husband! It's natural to feel a little awkward standing in front of someone you've never met who's holding a camera. But not to worry, my goal is to make everyone feel comfortable and to forget I'm even there. I don't want you to worry about how your kids are acting or what you're supposed to do. Leave it to me to make sure your gallery is filled with genuine belly laughs, tight squeezes, tender moments and lots of fun! I'll give you prompts so don't feel awkward, make sure you're in the most flattering light and suggest games to play with the kids so everyone’s having fun. No stiff poses or forced smiles here!

What to wear?

Something you feel fabulous in! But stick to a color palette instead of being too matchy matchy. It's always a toss up between jeans and a favorite t-shirt or an oh so pretty maxi dress for me!. Remember, l'm ways just a text or phone call away if you get stuck. And, I just happen to be an expert online shopper so will happily send you links to some of my favorite shops.

Where will the shoot take place? Inside or outside? At our home or another location? Yikes! My home isn't picture perfect!

Wherever location you choose, believe me, it will be the perfect place because you'll be with your favorite people. I have to say though, I love sessions at home, they're my absolute favorite. It's your space, where everyone feels their best and where your life happens. And don't worry, this is honest photography we're documenting your life right now in this moment, so don't stress if you're house isn't perfect. I get it! Have I told you yet that I have 3 little boys and 2 large furry dogs? A messy house and kids go hand in hand in my opinion and I assure you, that doesn't mean we won’t be able to find a perfect spot for your family to love on each other. But we can always venture outside, front steps or blankets in the backyard are also pretty amazing. Even cuddled on the sidewalk curb is fab! A favorite ice cream or coffee shop is pretty great too, we just need to figure out what type of documentary session you're after and plan from there.
